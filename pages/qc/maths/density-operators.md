$$

\newcommand{\ket}[1]{\left| #1 \right>}
\newcommand{\bk}[2]{\left< #1 \middle| #2 \right>}

## Definition
* Density operator of a pure state: $$\rho_x^X = \bra{x} \ket{x}$$

## Properties
* Density operators are positive
* Density operators have a trace of 1
