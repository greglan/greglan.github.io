## Classical Fourier transform
* Unitary matrix: $$F_n = \left( \frac{1}{\sqrt{n}} \omega_n^{pq} \right)_{1 \leqslant p,q \leqslant n}$$ where $$\omega_n = e^{2i \pi / n}$$ is the $$n$$-th root of unity

  Remark: for $$n=2$$, $$F_2 = H$$ the Hadamard matrix
